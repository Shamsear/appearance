Video Upload

Upload a match video to automatically extract player appearances. The system will analyze player ratings screens from the video and mark players as having appeared.

Video should contain "Player Ratings: Home" and "Player Ratings: Away" screens.
The video will be analyzed to extract player appearance data and update the database.
How It Works
  1. Upload a match video - The video should contain player ratings screens.
  2. Select teams - Choose the home and away teams from the dropdown.
  3. Choose processing type:
    • Automatic Processing - System automatically detects and updates player appearances.
    • Review First - Review extracted frames and confirm player selections before updating the database.
  4. Automatic processing - The system will:
    • Extract frames from the video
    • Identify frames showing player ratings
    • Use OCR to extract player names
    • Match extracted names with players in the database
    • Mark players as having appeared in the match
  5. View results - After processing, you'll see which players were detected and matched.
